What I can do

Web Development

Development is more than just being able to code. Every developer has the responsibility of upholding practices that keep the Web accessible, progressive, and relevant, including:

  • Compliant with W3C (World Wide Web Consortium) standards
  • Cross-browser compatible
  • Mobile responsive
  • Fully accessible to individuals with disabilities
  • Optimized for Search Engines (SEO)
  • Prioritizes site speed with well-written code
  • Frequent troubleshooting, debugging, and quality control
Web Design

I have strong design instincts and often get creative inspiration from unusual sources. While my personal design style is a little offbeat, I'm happy to work with those that prefer a more traditional look. Regardless of visual preferences, the fundamentals of web design are the same:

  • Designed mobile-first
  • Layout of content should shift according to screen size
  • Easy to navigate
  • Keeps your product or business mission the focal point
  • Aesthetically pleasing to look at, but not distracting
  • Coordinating colors, fonts, and images that improve readability of content
Logos + Vector Illustration

Choosing a logo for your business is a big decision, so I'm happy to provide several revisions and extensive collaboration. I use Adobe Illustrator, the industry standard, for all graphic design work:

  • Vector (infinitely scalable) illustrations that can be used for nearly anything you can think of
  • Custom logos with several alternate layouts, file types, and sizes
  • Redesign or modernization of an existing logo
  • Conversion of an existing non-vector logo (.jpg, .png, etc.) to vector format
